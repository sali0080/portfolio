About

Hey there, I'm Kaline Salibi

I'm embarking on an exhilarating journey across the realms of web development, digital graphics, and photography. Currently delving into my first year at Algonquin College,I've been immersing myself in these dynamic fields for about a year, eager to unlock their infinite possibilities.

In the world of photography, I find solace and inspiration. It’s more than just freezing moments in time, it’s an art form where creativity intertwines with courage. From heartfelt family moments to the subtle beauty of everyday life, my approach is guided by kindness and empathy, creating a safe space for authenticity to thrive.

These realms offer boundless opportunities for creativity and innovation, and I’m eager to explore their intersections with photography. I’m excited to weave my vision across these disciplines, crafting visual stories that resonate deeply with the hearts and minds of others.

By fostering a safe and empathetic environment, she helps clients feel comfortable and authentic during photography sessions, resulting in genuine and heartfelt moments captured.
